Safety Tips

Always agree to have your first personal meeting in a crowded public place such as a coffee shop or a mall. Please attend the meeting with the knowledge of your family or friends. Never agree to meet an unknown person in a secluded place or hotel room alone.

It is advisable to keep your family informed about any such meeting before your marriage and involve them in any decision taking process. It is always a wise move to have a family meeting, while deciding on a prospective bride or groom.

Always make sure that you are communicating with the right person with a real intention to get married. Please handle people who answer you vaguely with care. Never give your personal information such as credit card or bank account details to any one.

Be careful in dealing with the fraudsters and scammers who try to misuse the website to swindle money from you..
