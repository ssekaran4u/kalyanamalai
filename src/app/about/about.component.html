Welcome to Kalyanamalai Private Limited

Kalyanamalai Private Limited , was started in 1999 and shot to fame with its unique TV program, Kalyanamalai.

The company offers matrimonial services to Indians and Non Resident Indian's. Matrimonial alliances are arranged through the television programme, magazines and website We also arrange events and exclusive community meetings for prospective brides and grooms.

Kalyanamalai - SUN TV Program

Kalyanamalai is a unique matrimonial television programme telecast by SUN TV (a popular Tamil TV Channel) every Sunday between 08:00 - 08:30 AM.. This innovative programme is the brainchild of Mrs. Meera Nagarajan, the Managing Director & CEO of the company. She is also the director of the T.V.series It is anchored by T.V. Mohan who fondly came to be known as ‘’Kalyanamalai Mohan’’.

Through this programme, prospective brides and grooms accompanied by their parents or guardians are introduced and it is heartening to note that many of them have found their life partners here.

Kalyanamalai is viewed by millions of Tamilians all over the world. It is hugely popular and it is kept alive with subjective debates blending into the show, without being monotonous. The anchor, Mr. Mohan's presentation style has captured the audience and won him fans from all over the world. Some episodes are shot overseas like Malaysia, Singapore,Dubai, Doha and the USA. Needless to say it is now running into it's 700th episode.

Kalyanamalai Website

www.kmmatrimony.com is the web face of Kalyanamalai Private Limited. The website hosts profiles of millions of prospective and eligible brides and grooms from all communities.

Profiles with relevant details and photos are available for the website users. The systematically arranged profiles with details, makes the search for brides and grooms easy. The website currently receives over 5.3 lakh hits per day.The demand from the users of the website is continually growing.

At organized events, prospective brides and grooms register and a database with their profile and photo is created. These registered members receive profiles similar to their requirements to pick their choice.

Specially Arranged Matrimonial Programs

Kalyanamalai Private Limited regularly organises matrimonial events such as Thirumana Thiruvizha, Varan Parkalam Vanga, and Subha Muhurtha Dhina Sirappu registration. The events are organised under the Kalyanamalai banner and held all over India. Such events assist in easy selection for its participants.

All the new registrants at the organized events will immediately receive a set of matching profiles, complete with photo, horoscope, profile and contact particulars. After including all the newly registered profiles in the database, a fully compatible profile list matching the exact matrimonial preferences of the new user is sent to him/her. This unique feature further improves the matrimonial chances of all the registrants of Kalyanamalai.
